Privacy Policy for Unispace Interiors

At Unispace Interiors, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our website http://www.unispaceinteriors.in/.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information (such as name, email address, phone number)
  • Demographic information (such as location)
  • Payment information (if applicable for services)
  • Technical data (such as IP address, browser type, and usage information)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Communicate with you about your inquiries and requests
  • Process payments and complete transactions
  • Enhance our website and user experience
  • Send promotional emails (if you have opted in to receive them)

3. How We Protect Your Information

We use industry-standard security measures to safeguard your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We will not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website and providing our services, provided that these parties agree to keep your information confidential.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small data files stored on your device to track usage patterns and provide a more personalized experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites. These external sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for the content or practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing any personal information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@unispaceinteriors.in.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top of the policy. Please review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your personal information.
